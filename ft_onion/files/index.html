üîê SSH Access Information

To connect via SSH through Tor:

torify ssh root@<your-onion-address> -p 4242

Or configure your SSH client with Tor proxy:

ssh -o "ProxyCommand nc -X 5 -x 127.0.0.1:9050 %h %p" root@<your-onion-address> -p 4242
